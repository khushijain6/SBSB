Shree Bharat Saree Bhandar

Our business specializes in offering a wide range of sarees, including Banarsi sarees, Surat printed sarees, and embroidered sarees.

Banarsi sarees are known for their rich and intricate designs woven with gold or silver threads on silk fabric, originating from the city of Varanasi in India. These sarees exude elegance and opulence, making them a popular choice for special occasions and weddings.

Surat printed sarees are crafted using the traditional method of screen printing in the city of Surat, renowned for its textile industry. These sarees feature vibrant and colorful patterns and designs, making them trendy and suitable for both casual and festive wear.

Embroidered sarees are adorned with beautiful threadwork, sequins, beads, or other embellishments, adding a touch of glamour and artistry to the fabric. These sarees come in various styles and designs, catering to different tastes and preferences.

About Us

Bringing you an exquisite collection of sarees

These sarees are meticulously woven with intricate patterns, often using precious metallic threads that lend a touch of grandeur. Whether you're attending a wedding, a festive celebration, or any special occasion, our Banarsi sarees are sure to make you feel like a queen. Our Surat printed sarees are carefully crafted using the traditional technique of screen printing, these sarees feature eye-catching colors, bold motifs.

Perfect for everyday wear or a casual outing, our Surat printed sarees effortlessly blend comfort and style. For those seeking a touch of glamour and sophistication, our embroidered sarees are a testament to intricate craftsmanship. Each piece is embellished with delicate threadwork, shimmering sequins, or intricate beadwork, adding a radiant charm to the fabric. These sarees are ideal for parties, receptions, or any event where you want to stand out with your fashion statement.

We understand the significance of sarees as a symbol of tradition and cultural heritage. Our goal is to provide you with a diverse range of sarees that not only exemplify the rich textile traditions of India but also cater to the evolving fashion trends. We believe that every woman deserves to feel confident, beautiful, and comfortable in the saree of her choice.
